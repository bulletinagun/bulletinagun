How to do some things that are not there normally:

I was able to figure out only these which are needed by anyone & are not there...

1. We cannot calculate powers normally, but you may use the multiplication symbol 2 times for that purpose.

For Example: 9^4 will be 9**4 & there you go.

2. Some functions like "sec", "csc", "cot" are not there but for that, you will need to remember what equals to what.

For Example: sec(x) = 1/cos(x)

Some things that are there:

There is a Blank Space given for Noting things, that can be useful for so many things...you can copy the number from main screen & paste it here...

Example with Procedure:

You want to get sec(5);

You will insert 5;

Click on cos;

Copy the number;

Paste it in noting area;

Clear main area;

Insert 1/ ;

Insert the same thing in main area by seeing your Note;

Done!

Hidden Things & Others...

1. You can Switch from Deg to Rad & Rad to Deg, Just click on it!

It will only affect sin, cos, tan, sin^-1, cos^-1, tan^-1

2. There are Shortcuts, Just Press Alt & either use Arrow Keys or Keep Pressing the letter that is underlined!

3. Of Course, Don't think you ran Calculator & there came the screen with few buttons, that's it! There are very few things, it will not take much time, you should come to know what all things are there & what all things you can do with it!

4. It Calculates as per, Bracket then Exponent then Division then Multiplication then Addition then Subtraction...So, you will have to separate everything with brackets in the way you want them to be Calculated.
